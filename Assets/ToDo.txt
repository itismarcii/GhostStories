#########################################################
#### Mechanics #########################################
#########################################################

	Character:	Can hold two Items at once
				Eache Item in hand can be used/activated
			
				Walkie-Talkie
				Walk - Run - "Sneak" - "Crouch"
				Stamina System
					Air Recovery (Couldown after reaching Stamina Amount 0)
				Paranoia System
					Paranormal activities lead to sanity lose
					Light reduces sanity lose
					Complette darkness leads to sanity lose
					(Walkie-Talkie) Connection lose leads to minore sanity lose



	Ghost:		Walk - Teleport - Idles - Kill
				Modes
					Passive - Present - Active - Aggressive - Hunting
						Passive: None
						Present: Lights flicker, ...
						Active: Sound, Appearance Flicker, ...
						Aggressive: Throws Objects around, Doors clamp, Appearance for few sec, Player charge into disapearance ...
						Hunting: Appearance in middle range, disappearance in very low range (few sec later death of player), telportation
				Navemesh
					Door encounter ??
				Turns of lights walking past them

	Objects:	Lighter:		Activate:	Turn on Lighter
								Deactivate: Turn off Lighter
								Refileable:	True

				Lighter Fuel:	Activate:	Lighter in hand <- Fuel Lighter
								Deactivate:	-
								Refilable:	False

				Light Bulb:		Activate:	Puts Lighter Bulb into Position / Turns Lighter Bulb on
								Deactivate: Turns Lighter Bulb of if it has a Position
								Refilable:	False

				Ghost Radio:	Activate:	Turns Sound on
								Deactivate:	Turns Sound off
								Refilable:	True -> Batteries

				Batteries:		Activate:	Puts Batteries into other Hand object
								Deactivate:	-
								Refilable:	False
